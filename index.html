About Me

Hello! I'm Lon Nara

I'm a passionate UX/UI designer with a years of experience crafting intuitive and engaging digital experiences for businesses and individuals. My expertise spans user research, wireframing, prototyping, and visual design, allowing me to design end-to-end user experiences that are both functional and visually appealing.

My journey in UX/UI design began during my computer science degree, where I developed a strong foundation in human-centered design and usability principles. Since then, I’ve continuously learned and adapted to new design trends, tools, and methodologies. I believe in creating user-friendly, accessible interfaces backed by thoughtful design decisions.

When I’m not designing, you can find me exploring nature, reading UX case studies and tech blogs, or experimenting with new design tools and interaction patterns. I’m always open to new opportunities and creative collaborations.
